## 1. Headline Recommendation

**Winner:** `AI Consultant & Operations Lead | AI Strategy, Governance & Hands-On Automation | SC + NPPV3 Cleared`

**Rationale:** Research confirmed "Operational AI Consultant" is a niche title used by only 1 prominent UK practitioner. Recruiters overwhelmingly search for "AI Consultant" + "Operations" or "AI Transformation Lead". This hybrid headline:
- Front-loads "AI Consultant" (highest-searchable title — 87 active UK roles)
- Adds "Operations Lead" (matches recruiter boolean: `"AI Consultant" AND "Operations"`)
- Includes "Governance" (top 2026 keyword cluster — EU AI Act, Responsible AI)
- "Hands-On Automation" (Archetype E differentiator — you build it, not just advise it)
- SC + NPPV3 (recruiter filter keyword — 21.84% of roles require it)

**Current headline:** "Operational AI Consultant | AI Strategy & Hands-On Automation Delivery | SC + NPPV3 Cleared"  
**Change needed:** Replace with the hybrid above. ✓ Already partially matches — just needs "AI Consultant" first.

## 5. Power Content Ideas (3 Posts)

**Strategy per video:** Hook → Rehook → Body → CTA. Post frequency: 2-3x/week for 30 days to force LinkedIn algorithm lift.

### Post 1: "I Built a WhatsApp Bot That Closed Service Calls"

**Hook:** "I replaced a 12-person service desk team with a WhatsApp bot. Here's exactly how."

**Rehook:** "And no, I'm not a software engineer. I'm an ops guy who got tired of watching people do work a bot could do in 3 seconds."

**Body:** Walk through the Atos WhatsApp bot story — the problem (NHS service desk flooded with password resets and asset queries), the build (vision AI to read asset tags, WhatsApp API to auto-close tickets), the result (80% reduction in L1 call volume, team redeployed to higher-value work). Connect: "This is what I mean by AI-enabled operations — not theory, not slide decks. A WhatsApp bot that actually shipped."

**CTA:** "What manual process in your organisation is begging for a bot? Drop it in the comments — I'll tell you if it's automatable in a weekend."

### Post 2: "SC Cleared + AI Consultant = Rare Combination"

**Hook:** "21.84% of UK AI Consultant jobs require SC clearance. And yet most AI consultants can't get cleared."

**Rehook:** "I hold both SC and NPPV3. Here's why that matters for government AI projects — and why you should care even if you don't work in government."

**Body:** The reality of AI in regulated environments — data handling policies, audit trails, access controls, EU AI Act compliance. Most AI consultants build in unregulated sandboxes; Russell has delivered in NATO-aligned, NHS, and UK Government environments where security is non-negotiable. This isn't a limitation — it's the entire point of operational AI governance.

**CTA:** "If your organisation needs AI that works inside security boundaries, message me. I operate where most consultants can't."

### Post 3: "The AI Operating Model Nobody Talks About"

**Hook:** "Every company is racing to 'do AI'. But 82% of AI POCs never make it to production."

**Rehook:** "The problem isn't the AI. It's the operating model. You can't layer AI on top of a broken process and expect magic."

**Body:** Use the Comunica (weeks-to-days deployment compression) and Lambeth (60% cost reduction through workflow redesign) stories — reframed as early evidence of what's now called "AI operating model design". The process was the problem before AI existed; AI just made the fix faster. The skill is knowing which processes to fix first.

**CTA:** "Repost if you've seen an AI POC die because the process underneath was broken. Follow for more on operational AI — the stuff that actually ships."
