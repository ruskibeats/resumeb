## Role Summary

De-facto Operations & Infrastructure Director across EMEA for 4 years. Accountable for a live portfolio of 15+ concurrent operational programmes serving Tier 1 finance, UK Government, NATO and NHS clients. Combined delivery leadership with commercial lifecycle management, vendor governance, and executive reporting.

## Key Responsibilities & Activities

**Operational Delivery:**
- Managed delivery of multi-site infrastructure projects across Europe, leading geographically dispersed technical teams as primary client-facing delivery lead.
- Planned and delivered infrastructure projects in line with agreed scope, timelines, budgets, and quality standards.
- Led stakeholder engagement, requirements definition, and delivery planning across multiple countries and vendors.
- Managed risks, dependencies, and delivery constraints, removing blockers to maintain programme momentum.
- Established clear governance, reporting, and communication rhythms across delivery teams and clients.

**Commercial & Operational Governance:**
- Owned end-to-end commercial lifecycle: procurement, SLA governance, billing and inventory capture, vendor performance across 130-site rollouts.
- Maintained operational continuity across 6,100+ circuit migrations and 7,000+ endpoint deployments; managed cutover risk and service-continuity obligations.
- Governed operational risk and compliance across SD-WAN/SASE migrations in regulated environments including NATO-aligned and NHS infrastructure.
- Tracked cost-to-serve, managed vendor SLA performance and produced executive reporting from engineering teams to C-Suite stakeholders.

**M&A / Integration:**
- Led M&A integration, network refresh, EUC and datacentre migration programmes.
