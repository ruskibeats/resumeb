## Role Summary

Programme lead for multi-site technology modernisation programmes across large, live operational estates. Delivered EUC and network upgrades within high-occupancy, customer-facing environments — including NHS Trust estates — with strict zero-disruption constraints.

---

## Key Responsibilities & Activities

- Led engineering teams across multiple live sites, coordinating site readiness, access sequencing, night works and change windows within occupied buildings.
- Managed network uplifts including LAN, Wi-Fi and comms-room upgrades, working alongside facilities, M&E and principal contractors during in-occupation works.
- Acted as senior accountable delivery interface between client stakeholders, delivery partners, vendors and programme governance.
- Led early-stage discovery and readiness assessments across Trust estates, shaping deployment sequencing, risk positions, and executive recommendations for Windows 11 and network modernisation.
- Improved delivery throughput and operational stability across mission-critical, always-on estates.
- Delivered upgrades within strict zero-disruption constraints, maintaining continuous operations throughout live environments.
