## Role Summary

Appointed to design, structure, and deliver a greenfield digital gaming and high-performance infrastructure programme supporting Las Vegas Sands' entry into online gaming in Europe. Operated at country-level Operations Director / MD level — ran the P&L, inherited the in-country team, and led the full programme from discovery through to live operational handover.

This was a first-of-its-kind deployment for Las Vegas Sands in Europe.

---

## Key Responsibilities & Activities

**Programme / Delivery:**
- Led end-to-end discovery and programme mobilisation, defining scope, sequencing, governance, and delivery approach for a first-of-its-kind online gaming platform.
- Advised senior stakeholders on architecture options, delivery risk, regulatory constraints, and commercial trade-offs.
- Orchestrated multi-vendor delivery across data centres, low-latency networks, security, cloud integration, and operational readiness.
- Acted as executive assurance point between engineering teams, vendors, and global stakeholders across Europe, Asia, and the US.
- Established sustainable operational handover models, support structures, and performance baselines.

**Operations / P&L:**
- Held full P&L and commercial accountability for the multi-jurisdiction, multi-vendor programme.
- Owned complete contract-to-cash cycle: vendor contracts, SLA frameworks, billing accuracy, commercial event management and revenue assurance from operational launch.
- Managed day-to-day operations: 24-engineer multilingual team, live service performance, vendor SLA compliance, escalation management.
- Built and chaired operational governance framework: weekly service reviews, Board-level KPI packs covering performance, risk, compliance and commercial position.
- Accountable for managed infrastructure estate spanning 1,200+ gaming endpoints, primary data centre, seven IDCs and AWS-integrated connectivity.
- Designed operational handover model and performance baselines transitioning platform into sustainable live service.
