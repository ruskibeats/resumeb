## Role Summary

Delivered complex digital and infrastructure transformation programmes across UK Central Government and enterprise clients, operating in both delivery leadership and pre-sales support roles. Operated under GDS (Government Digital Service) standards, covering cloud, networking, procurement, and operational governance frameworks.

---

## Key Responsibilities & Activities

**Delivery Leadership:**
- Led programme delivery across Home Office, MHCLG (Ministry of Housing, Communities & Local Government), MoJ (Ministry of Justice), FSA, Ofsted, PINS and NHS — supporting large-scale, high-visibility public-sector initiatives.
- Delivered Microsoft 365, identity and EUC transformations at national scale (Home Office ~42,000 users; MHCLG ~5,000 users), including migration planning, security alignment and operational transition.
- Managed procurement strategy, design assurance and delivery coordination across networking, cloud, EUC and virtualisation platforms.
- Delivered GDS-aligned discovery and alpha workshops, business cases and delivery roadmaps, ensuring compliance with central government assurance frameworks.
- Acted as trusted interface between technical teams, commercial leads and client sponsors, balancing delivery feasibility with bid competitiveness and contractual commitments.

**Pre-Sales / Bid Support:**
- Authored and contributed to bid responses, RFP submissions and commercial proposals, translating technical delivery approaches into clear client value propositions.
- Supported bid presentations and client-facing workshops, articulating delivery methodology, governance models, risk controls and commercial assumptions to senior stakeholders.
