## Role Summary

Contract Lead Enterprise Architect and de-facto technology operations lead for a 5,000-employee local authority. Initially contracted on the GCSx project (Government Connect Secure Extranet), then retained and promoted to Lead Enterprise Architect. Delivered the authority's most significant LAN/WAN, telephony, and infrastructure transformation programmes over a sustained 7-year engagement.

---

## Key Responsibilities & Activities

**Initial Contract Role (Mar 2009–Feb 2010 — Network Project Manager):**
- Led the upgrade of Council systems and supported ICT infrastructure to maintain compliance with DWP (Department for Work and Pensions) Benefits Processing Standards.
- Delivered GCSx (Government Connect Secure Extranet) compliance project.

**Lead Enterprise Architect (Feb 2010–Apr 2015):**
- Led authority-wide LAN/WAN and telephony transformation programmes across 140+ Council sites.
- Delivered 60% cost reduction across 200+ sites through strategic procurement and network redesign.
- Led £12M voice and data services re-tender — the largest single commercial programme in the engagement.
- Deployed telephony infrastructure supporting 5,000 employees across distributed public sector environment.
- Owned operational governance, SLA management and vendor performance across a multi-year transformation programme.
- Established telephony and wide-area network infrastructure as a managed, governed service.
