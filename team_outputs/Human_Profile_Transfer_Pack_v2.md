## PART G: COMPETITIVE POSITIONING FOR HIGH-APPLICANT DIRECTOR POOLS

When posting has high director/CXO applicant volume:

1. **Open with quantified proof stack** (not biography)
2. **Show mandate complexity** (regulated + multi-site + commercial + governance)
3. **Demonstrate operating mechanism** (how you run programmes, not just outcomes)
4. **Use credibility controls** (qualified wording on Medium/Low confidence claims)
5. **Avoid title inflation** (competitors are screened for factual consistency)
6. **Differentiate via evidence density across 3 axes:** delivery scale, commercial control, governance assurance

Suggested top-of-CV “proof strip” format:
- 6,100+ circuits managed | 7,000+ endpoints delivered | 25,000 Wi-Fi endpoints deployed
- 60% WAN cost reduction | £12m re-tender led | 42,000-user M365 migration
- SC-cleared | government, healthcare, defence, finance
