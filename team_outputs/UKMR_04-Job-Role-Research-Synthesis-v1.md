### ROLE 1: OPERATIONS DIRECTOR — UK Tech / Managed Services / Data Centre / Cabling

#### 3.1.1 Core Mandate

The Operations Director owns the end-to-end operational performance of a technology business unit, managed service, MSP, or infrastructure estate. In the data centre and cabling sectors specifically, this role has additional dimensions:

- **Data centre context:** Responsible for operational reliability of physical infrastructure (power, cooling, cabling, connectivity), managed hosting, and colocation services. 24/7 operations governance, maintenance regime oversight, and critical facilities management awareness.
- **Cabling / structured cabling context:** Oversight of installation operations, field engineering workforce management, quality assurance, and project delivery pipeline. Often combined with P&L accountability across a service delivery portfolio.
- **MSP / managed services context:** SLA governance, service desk management, transition-to-service ownership, and contract-to-cash lifecycle.

**Primary accountabilities (universal):**
- End-to-end operational delivery model: staffing, structure, tooling, SLAs
- P&L or significant opex accountability
- Vendor and partner SLA management
- Executive/board-level KPI reporting
- Commercial governance (contract renewals, renegotiations, NPS)
- Team leadership (20–200+ headcount)
- Operational risk and compliance

**Confidence: High**
