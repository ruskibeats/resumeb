# Research: LinkedIn SEO Keywords & Recruiter Search Phrases — UK AI Consulting Roles

**Target market:** UK-based contract/consultant roles for Russell Batchelor — 30-year infrastructure/ops veteran pivoting to AI consulting. Holds SC + NPPV3 clearance.
**Date:** 8 May 2026

---

## Summary

"Operational AI Consultant" is a niche title used by a handful of practitioners (e.g. Mike Wood) but is **not a commonly searched recruiter keyword**. Recruiters overwhelmingly search for "AI Consultant", "Head of AI", "AI Operations Manager", and "AI Transformation Lead" as discrete titles. To maximise LinkedIn discoverability, Russell's headline should lead with **"AI Consultant & AI Operations Lead"** (the two highest-volume searchable titles) and embed operational/transformation/governance keywords in the summary and skills sections. SC/NPPV3 clearance is a significant differentiator — 21.84% of AI Consultant job ads in the UK require SC clearance, making it a searchable keyword recruiters actively filter on [IT Jobs Watch].

## Findings

### 1. "Operational AI Consultant" — Niche title, low search volume. Use hybrid approach.

**Verdict: Do NOT use as primary headline. Use as a descriptor in the summary.**

- Only one prominent UK practitioner (Mike Wood, michaeljwood.com) uses this exact title. His positioning is "I help UK businesses turn AI into a practical everyday tool" — operations-adjacent but not a commonly searched job title [Mike Wood].
- No recruiter on LinkedIn uses "Operational AI Consultant" as a boolean search term. Recruiters typically search: `"AI Consultant" AND "Operations"` or `"AI Consultant" AND "Operational Excellence"`.
- In UK job boards, the closest match is "Operational Excellence Consultant (AI)" — Reinvigoration has contractor roles at this title across London, Liverpool, Sheffield [AI Jobs UK].
- **Recommendation:** Use **"AI Consultant & Operations Lead"** as the LinkedIn headline. Add "Operational AI" as a keyword in the About section. Embed "AI Operations" and "Operational Excellence" as skills.

**LinkedIn boolean string recruiters use:**  
`("AI Consultant" OR "Artificial Intelligence Consultant") AND ("Operations" OR "Operational Excellence" OR "Process Improvement")`

### 2. "Head of AI Operations" / "AI Operations" — Active, well-searched titles

**Verdict: High-value target role. Use variations in headline and skills.**

- **Head of AI Operations (b2b SaaS, London):** £145K-£195K. Requires AI strategy, AI governance, automation workflows, CRM/support integration, predictive insights. Skills listed: AI strategy, Agentic AI, Data Governance, Automation platforms [AI Jobs Net].
- **Senior AI Operations Manager (Mace Consult, London):** £70K-£90K. Owns end-to-end operation of AI capability. Blends DevOps discipline with governance. Requires: AI platforms, DevOps, stakeholder management [Experteer / StudySmarter].
- **AI Operations Project Consultant (Contract/FTC, London):** £55K-£62K. Focus: automate admin workflows, AI-enabled reporting, build AI ops roadmap, develop playbooks [AI Jobs Net].
- **Senior Manager, AI Operations (IRIS International):** Permanent. End-to-end GenAI delivery, scoping opportunities, building/deploying tools, process improvement [IRIS Current Vacancies].
- **AI Operations Lead (M-KOPA, Manchester):** First hire. Design, ship, scale automations and AI agents [Built In Manchester].
- **Principal AI Operations Lead (Microsoft UK):** Cloud quality, security, reliability focus [Flexa Careers].

**Key skills demanded across these roles:**

## SC Clearance as a Differentiator

- **21.84% of UK AI Consultant job ads** specifically require SC clearance [IT Jobs Watch].
- **Active SC-cleared AI roles found:**
  - **Principal AI Strategy Consultant (SC Clearance, London):** £110K-£130K + bonus — Defence and National Security, lead complex advisory engagements [TotalJobs].
  - **AI Product Lead (SC/DV Cleared):** £600-£700/day contract — UK government AI/data/digital transformation [La Fosse].
  - **AI Engineer (SC Cleared):** £600/day Outside IR35, remote — AI platform for secure/regulated environments [IO Associates].
  - **AI Code Assurance Engineer (SC/DV Cleared):** London/Manchester/Bristol — secure government AI programme [La Fosse].
- NPPV3 (Non-Police Personnel Vetting) is rarer than SC but valuable for public sector / police-adjacent AI consulting.
- **Recommendation:** Put "SC Cleared" in the LinkedIn headline and Summary. Recruiters filter by it. Use `"AI Consultant" AND "SC Cleared"` in boolean strings.

The assessment is based on: (a) job posting volume for each title, (b) practitioner presence, (c) recruiter boolean string templates. - Contract rate data for SC-cleared AI roles is sparse outside the 3-4 postings found. A dedicated search on SecurityClearedJobs (subscription) or LinkedIn Recruiter with clearance filter would yield more granular data.
- NPPV3-specific roles were not found in this search. This is a niche clearance. May need to cross-reference with police/Home Office AI procurement roles separately.
- UK-specific data on "AI Governance" vs "AI Strategy" keyword volume was not available from IT Jobs Watch — the site tracks "Artificial Intelligence Consultant" as one category and doesn't break out governance sub-roles.
