## Scoring Dimensions

Score each dimension independently on a 0-100 scale. Be specific and evidence-based in your rationale. **Each dimension below already excludes hidden items per the Hidden Items Rule. Do not reference hidden items in any dimension's rationale or evidence.**

Since no target job description is available, score the resume purely on ATS quality and general CV strength.

1. **Clarity & Specificity** — Evaluate only visible bullet points. Are they concrete? Do they use action verbs? Is it clear what the candidate did and what level they operated at? Ignore hidden entries entirely.

2. **Impact & Quantification** — Evaluate only visible achievements. Are they quantified with scale (sites, users, budget, team size, cost savings, timelines)? Are outcomes described or just activities? Hidden entries are excluded.

3. **ATS Compatibility** — Evaluate only visible content. Does the resume use industry-standard terminology? Are there duplicate visible skill names, missing dates, or inconsistent formatting? **Hidden items are intentionally excluded from ATS output and must not be mentioned.**

4. **Structure & Completeness** — Evaluate only visible sections. Are all major sections (Summary, Experience, Education, Skills) present and populated? Layout clean and readable? Certifications, clearances properly listed? Hidden sections are irrelevant here.

5. **Language Quality & Relevance** — Evaluate only visible text. Professional tone, consistent voice, no cliches or inflated language. Experience descriptions match the implied seniority level.
