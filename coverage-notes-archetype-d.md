## Gap Analysis — Honest Limitations

### 1. No hands-on ML engineering credentials
**Gap:** Many "Head of AI" roles expect Python, ML pipeline experience, or MLOps tooling.
**Mitigation:** CV is correctly positioned as *operational AI leadership* not engineering. Headline and profile are explicit. Unlikely to fool a technically literate screener but correctly positions Russell for operational/strategic AI leadership roles, not engineering leadership roles.
**Recommendation:** If a JD emphasises technical AI depth, add a brief clarifying statement in cover letter: "I lead AI strategy and adoption at the operating model level; I partner with engineering teams rather than replacing them."

### 2. AI ROI figures are directional, not audited
**Gap:** The CV references "measurable bottom-line efficiency gains" and "quantified operational pain points" but does not name exact figures from the PE portfolio AI work (per brief instruction: do not claim exact AI ROI figures).
**Mitigation:** Acceptable — the Lambeth 60% WAN figure and £12M re-tender provide the quantification credibility the CV needs. AI-specific metrics are qualitative by design.

### 3. Current AI engagements are recent (Jun 2025 onward)
**Gap:** The Independent Consultant AI role starts Jun 2025 — relatively recent. Some JDs will look for 3–5 years of AI strategy experience.
**Mitigation:** The PE portfolio engagement starts Mar 2020 and carries the AI automation framing. The career narrative (30 years of process automation) is the real counter-argument. Coach Russell to use this framing verbally in interviews.

### 4. No named AI tool stack
**Gap:** CV does not name specific AI tools (ChatGPT, Copilot, Claude, n8n, Zapier, etc.).
**Mitigation:** Intentional — avoids claims that can be probed at interview unless Russell is genuinely fluent. If Russell has specific tools he has used confidently, a short "AI Tooling" line in Key Skills could be added (e.g., "OpenAI / Copilot for M365 / n8n workflow automation / prompt engineering").
**Action:** Confirm with Russell which tools he has genuine hands-on exposure to before adding.

### 5. Atos and Collective IP engagements are dated Sept/Jun 2025
**Gap:** Both are presented as concurrent with the CV date of May 2026 — this is consistent with the task brief and likely accurate, but should be confirmed with Russell before submission.

### 6. No formal AI certification
**Gap:** No AI-specific certification (e.g., Microsoft AI-900, Google AI Essentials, or similar).
**Mitigation:** Not a blocker for a Head of AI Operations role. If Russell obtains one (quick to do), add to Certifications. Recommend Microsoft AI-900 or Google AI Essentials as fast, credible additions.
